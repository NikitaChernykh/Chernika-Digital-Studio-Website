# Design Guidelines - Chernika Digital

## Aesthetic Stance: Kinetic

Motion-forward, dynamic energy. Dark canvas with light motion highlights, precise animations, and bold typography. Inspired by modern product reveals and digital-first studios.

## Typography

**Display**: Outfit (geometric sans, bold weights for headings)
**Body**: Inter (clean, readable for descriptions and body copy)

## Color Tokens

**Ground**: Deep dark slate (#0a0a0f) with subtle gradient mesh
**Accent**: Electric blue (#3b82f6) for interactive elements and emphasis
**Highlights**: Bright white (#ffffff) for motion trails and key content
**Muted**: Slate gray (#64748b) for secondary text

## Layout Principles

- Asymmetric grid compositions with intentional motion paths
- Generous whitespace to let content breathe
- Layered depth with subtle shadows and overlays
- Section transitions feel kinetic and purposeful

## Visual Treatment

- Dark-on-light reversed to light-on-dark for modern feel
- Gradient mesh backgrounds for depth
- Thin hairline borders in low opacity
- Hover states with subtle scale and glow
- Motion hints: subtle parallax, fade-ins, scale on scroll

## Imagery

- High-contrast product shots
- Real project screenshots
- Context-specific Unsplash photos with dark overlays when needed
- Maintain aspect ratios, use object-fit carefully

## Responsive Behavior

- Desktop-first with intentional mobile collapse
- Breakpoint at ~1024px for grid restructuring
- Touch-friendly interactive elements on mobile
- Preserve kinetic feel across viewports
